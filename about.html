About Me

I started working in a Lan House when I was only 12 years old, there I spent my free time, repairing computers, configuring and reinstalling the system, it was during this period that a great passion for computers grew and I began to understand the power that the internet made possible.

Some time later, in mid-2013, I started to develop personal projects using Arduino, a microcontroller board that accepts C language, that's when I got a good experience with programming and the Internet of Things, and below is a preview of one of the projects I executed with Arduino:

E foi então que veio a ideia de criar meu primeiro canal no youtube, onde eu postava minhas gambiarras e ensinava como eu executava meus projetos

Time passed and I started working in a Bank, that's right, it didn't have much to do with my passion but it was something that I saw as an opportunity to improve my life, I took this opportunity and today I am Manager of a unit of a large Bank, managing a team with more than 30 people and working on improving daily processes, although it is an area far from programming, I got vast experience with team management and always suggesting and applying improvements in daily processes, optimizing time.

In parallel with my work at Bank I was always present on the internet and delivering projects for free on youtube and on the Nerd King blog, some of these projects were even articles in local newspapers

With the Covid-19 pandemic, I realized that the world had an acceleration of changes, mainly regarding the development and application of technologies in the most varied jobs.

It was then that I planned a change of route in my life and started a new degree to professionalize my passion for technology and migrate my area of expertise in the market. And so at the beginning of 2022 I started to study "Analysis and Systems Development", then I come on an incessant journey to learn more and not limit myself to the conventional University, I'm doing several training at Alura, an amazing course platform that is helping me in this learning process

I am currently open to part-time "Front-End" jobs in a Home Office regime and I guarantee the credibility of a serious professional who will do the best for your project or company.
